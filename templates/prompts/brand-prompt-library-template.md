# Prompt Library

## Common Positive Prompt

Original LINE emoji / sticker character or motif for `[BRAND_NAME]`.
Simple readable silhouette, bold outline, clear expression or meaning, small-size readability, transparent background assumption.
Keep the brand-specific visual symbols and avoid unnecessary background detail.

## Negative Prompt

Existing character style, famous work, famous artist style, real person, company logo, real brand, violence, sexual expression, politics, religion, discrimination, self-harm, heavy dark expression, drug, gambling, advertising, URL, generated text, cluttered background, tiny details, thin outline, low contrast.

## Text Policy

Do not generate final submission text inside images. Add text after image generation.

## Candidate Policy

For production finalization, generate at least four candidates per item and compare by structure, brand fit, product use, and small-size readability.
