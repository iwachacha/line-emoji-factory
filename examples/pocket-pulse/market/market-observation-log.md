# Pocket Pulse Market Observation Log

## Scope
- Date: 2026-04-26
- Purpose: factory audit brand for dual static emoji and static sticker support.
- Market lens: daily coordination, short reaction, low-pressure work and family chat.

## Observation Summary
- Common reaction products often lean on loud emotion, mascot cuteness, or text-heavy phrases.
- Pocket Pulse intentionally uses a small signal mascot to express status without demanding attention.
- Emoji suitability: compact icons can sit inline as a status suffix.
- Sticker suitability: the same signal can become a larger single-send response with a short phrase.

## Structural Judgment
- Structure: Pass. The brand has eight high-frequency daily states and each state can be expressed without a long sentence.
- Brand: Pass. The round signal core, dark outline, and restrained status language remain stable across item types.
- Product: Pass. Emoji and sticker releases have different roles and do not force the brand to release both every time.

## Risk Notes
- Avoid corporate dashboard or notification-app styling.
- Avoid sale, campaign, free, or launch-date language.
- Avoid making SOS look violent or emergency-service affiliated; keep it as casual help-needed communication.
